1)The numerical version of the game is usually played with 4 digits.
2)the player writes a 4-digit secret number. 
3)The digits must be all different.
4)the players try to guess the number.
5)If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls".
6)If in different positions, they are "cows". 
Example:
Secret number: 4271
your guess: 1234
Answer: 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4" and "1".)
